Sora Stationery (空 / Sky)

Sora Stationery was born from a simple desire: to capture the quiet, fleeting beauty of a drifting cloud, a starry midnight, or a warm cup of barley tea, and press it into tactile paper and ink.

Our temporary Kyoto glasshouse pop-up gathers masterfully crafted instruments and fine papers from indie ateliers. We believe that putting physical pen to paper is a slow, meditative act of self-care—a sanctuary where thoughts can stretch and daydreams can settle.

For those who cannot visit our garden glasshouse in person, this virtual archive allows you to take home a piece of the sky. Each order is meticulously wrapped by hand with a pressed botanical leaf, wrapped in starry vellum paper, and hand-stamped with a vintage brass wax seal.
